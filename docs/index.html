How it works

  1. Scan

    Install the free A11yWatch browser extension and scan any page in one click. See accessibility issues in plain English — no setup, no account required.

  2. Monitor

    Connect a site and A11yWatch re-scans it on a schedule, tracking every issue over time so nothing slips through between updates.

  3. Get alerted

    When a deploy or a content change introduces a new issue, A11yWatch tells you — before your client, or their lawyer, notices.

    Generate a clean, branded PDF report for each client: your logo, your name, your recurring service.

About accessibility

Automated scanning catches many common accessibility issues, but it can't catch everything — some WCAG criteria require human judgment. A11yWatch is a monitoring, alerting, and documentation tool. It helps you find, track, and document accessibility issues; it does not guarantee legal compliance.
