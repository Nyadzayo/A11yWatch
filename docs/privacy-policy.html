A11yTrail - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how A11yTrail handles information across this website and the A11yTrail browser extension. In short: the extension collects no data, and we do not operate user accounts or collect personal information through this site.

1. The browser extension collects nothing.

The A11yTrail extension runs entirely in your browser. When you click it, it analyzes only the page you are actively viewing (using the activeTab and scripting permissions) and shows the results to you locally. Nothing is transmitted to us or any third party, nothing is stored on our servers, and there is no account or sign-in. The extension does not track your browsing history and does not read pages you do not explicitly scan. Reports you export are saved directly to your own device.

2. This website.

This is a static informational website. We do not run accounts, advertising, or analytics / tracking cookies on it. Our hosting provider may keep standard server request logs as described in their own privacy documentation; we do not use that data to identify or profile you.

3. What we don't do.

We do not sell or share personal information. We do not build advertising profiles, and we do not use third-party trackers.

4. Future services.

A11yTrail's hosted monitoring service is not yet available. If and when it launches, this policy will be updated to describe exactly what data that service processes before you use it.

5. Children.

The Service is not directed to anyone under 18.

6. Changes.

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
